## Update Your App

Follow these steps to ensure you run the latest version.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for Updates" icon="download">
    Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.

    Search for Shomri and tap **Update** if available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes" icon="book-open">
    After updating, check the in-app changelog under Settings > About.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Filtering" icon="refresh-cw">
    Toggle filtering off and on to apply new rules.

    Test with a known site to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">

    ```bash
    # Automatic updates recommended
    Settings > App Store > App Updates: On
    ```

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Troubleshooting" icon="help-circle">

    If updates fail:

    1. Sign out and back into App Store
    2. Restart device
    3. Contact support via `https://dashboard.example.com/support`

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
