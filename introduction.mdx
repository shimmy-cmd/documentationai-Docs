## Overview

Shomri provides reliable content filtering and device management for iOS devices. You gain safe browsing, video filtering, and app controls without compromising speed or usability. Trusted by over 500 families, Shomri ensures protection tailored to your needs, whether for personal use or family management.

Shomri solves common filtering frustrations like blocking essential apps or slow performance. You customize strictness levels and assign managers, such as parents or mentors, for seamless control.

## Quick Start

Get Shomri running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download" icon="download">
    Install Shomri from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create an account at [shomri.com/auth](https://shomri.com/auth). Use your email to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Filters" icon="settings">
    Choose your protection level: light, medium, or strict. Enable video scanning and app blocks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test It" icon="play-circle">
    Browse sites and apps to verify blocks work as expected.
  </Step>
</Steps>
