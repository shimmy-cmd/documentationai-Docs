## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- An iPhone or iPad running iOS 15 or later
- A stable internet connection
- An Apple ID for App Store downloads
- Optional: A friend or family member to act as your manager (Shomri supports self-management too)
</Callout>

Shomri works seamlessly on both iPhone and iPad. The setup process takes less than 5 minutes.

## Installation Steps

Follow these steps to get Shomri up and running.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Shomri Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [shomri.com/auth](https://shomri.com/auth) in your browser.
    
    Enter your email and create a password. Verify your email if prompted.
    
    Choose your management model: self-managed, parent-managed, or mentor-managed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download from App Store" icon="download">
    Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
    
    Search for "Shomri" and tap **Get** to download.
    
    Open the app and sign in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Setup Wizard" icon="settings">
    Grant necessary permissions: VPN configuration for filtering, notifications, and device management.
    
    Shomri will guide you through enabling profiles and trusting the developer certificate.
    
    Tap **Continue** through each screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Common Issues" default-open="false">
  
### VPN Won't Connect
- Ensure you're on iOS 15+
- Go to Settings > VPN & Device Management > Trust Shomri profile

### App Not Filtering
- Restart the Shomri app
- Toggle filtering off/on in settings

### Forgot Password
Reset via [shomri.com/auth](https://shomri.com/auth)

If issues persist, contact support through the app.
