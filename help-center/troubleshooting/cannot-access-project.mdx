---
title: Cannot Access Project Space
description: Quick fix if you can't access your Shomri documentation space.
---

## Symptoms

- You see a "Not Authorized" or "Project Not Found" message

## Possible Causes

- You are not added as a collaborator
- Project was deleted or archived

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check access">
    Ask a workspace admin to verify your membership for the space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Have the admin invite you via your email in Shomri's user management.
  </Step>
</Steps>
