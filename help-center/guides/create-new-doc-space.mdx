---

## Overview

Organize your project docs by creating a new space in Shomri.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open dashboard">
    Go to `https://shomri.com/dashboard` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create space">
    Click **New Documentation Space**. Fill in your project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team">
    Add collaborators by email as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    Begin adding pages and folders to organize your documents.
  </Step>
</Steps>
